Baddie Bomb Blobby (2025)

Baddie Bomb Blobby is an experimental 2D roguelike dating simulator designed to explore the integration of ChatGPT and Large Language Models (LLMs) in generating an artificial world.

The development challenge was to create a framework capable of dynamically generating 40 levels using AI techniques. The roguelike genre, known for procedural level generation, was chosen to emphasize the game's adaptability and AI-driven content.

The dating simulator component, being primarily text-based, allowed for real-time conversational interaction with ChatGPT. The 2D format was selected for its simplicity in generating environments at runtime.

Combining these elements, Baddie Bomb Blobby delivers a unique experience where procedurally generated gameplay and AI-driven dialogue blend seamlessly in a dynamically evolving narrative.

Pacman 3D (2024)

Pacman 3D is a 2D classic arcade game reimagined in stunning 3D. Navigate through the maze, eat pellets, avoid ghosts, and collect power-ups to clear each level. In this 3D version, you control Pac-Man as a 2D but in 3D such as walking in the mazes, eating pellets and avoiding the normal ghosts. Collect special power-up pellets to gain power and eat the ghosts once they become weak before they regain their power state.

Pacman: Is the hero Sphere who gets rid of the ghosts from the maze. He likes to eat when he is happy.

Ghosts: There are 3 colorful ghosts that will try to catch Pac-Man. If they touch Pac-Man, game over. Pac-Man defeats them by avoiding them or using power-ups to eat the ghosts.

Tips and Strategies:

  • Learn the Maze: Familiarize yourself with controls and stay away from the ghosts’ areas.
  • Use Corners to Your Advantage: The maze has corners and narrow paths. Use them to avoid ghosts and trap them when they are vulnerable.
  • Time Your Power Pellets: Don’t rush to eat a Power Pellet unless it’s safe. Wait for the ghosts to get close, then turn the tables.

Saving The Earth (2023)

Saving The Earth is a 2D shooting platformer game I developed using the GameMaker engine. The game features an enemy AI algorithm where enemies aim and shoot bullets directly at the player’s position, forcing players to stay on the move and avoid standing still for too long—otherwise, they will be hit quickly.

The story is set in a future where Earth is under threat from an alien invasion. Mars has already fallen, and Earth is next. The aliens seek to conquer Earth and steal its valuable natural resources. They refuse to share with humanity, viewing humans as greedy and unworthy of coexistence.
