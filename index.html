Saving The Earth (2023)

This is a 2D shooting platformer game I developed using the GameMaker engine. The game features an enemy AI algorithm where enemies aim and shoot bullets directly at the player’s position, forcing players to stay on the move and avoid standing still for too long—otherwise, they will be hit quickly. The story is set in a future where Earth is under threat from an alien invasion. Mars has already fallen, and Earth is next. The aliens seek to conquer Earth and steal its valuable natural resources. They refuse to share with humanity, viewing humans as greedy and unworthy of coexistence. In response, Earth sends a highly trained soldier to defend the planet and save humankind from the alien threat.
